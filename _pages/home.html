We are the premiere Ballroom Dance organization at the University of Southern California.

Come to any Monday or Wednesday class (times below) to try dancing — no experience is required and your first week is free!

Every year, we host our own competition, the Glamour Smackdown! Find the details at the Glamour Smackdown website.

What we do:

  • We hold classes for both beginners and experienced dancers in many styles, taught by professional teachers.
  • We compete across California and the U.S.
  • We go out social dancing around L.A. — Swing dancing, Salsa dancing, and more!
  • We perform at both on-campus and off-campus events.
  • We have lots of fun!
