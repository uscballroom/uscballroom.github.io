--- title: Join ---

Join the Team

We welcome all USC students, alumni, and faculty, as well as Los Angeles community members. Our team is for all experience levels, from total beginners to dancing masters. No tryouts needed.

Sign up for our email newsletter for updated practice dates and times, and our other events.

Come to one of our classes to try out ballroom dancing with no commitment.

Find us on Facebook for updates on our events and photos of our members at competitions!

Membership benefits:

  • Hour-long beginner and intermediate classes, 2 days a week, with professional coaches (see them on the leaders page)
  • Practice sessions where you can improve your dancing skills under the mentorship of experienced members
  • Carpools to social dancing events around the L.A. area
  • Discounts on dance shoes through our favorite shoe vendor, Crown Dance Shoes

Schedule

Classes are on Monday and Wednesday evenings. See the Schedule page for times and dates.

Membership cost

Try out ballroom dancing for first week for free! To join the team, there is a fee of $80 per semester for USC students. This fee allows us to have our incredible professional coaches teach us every week. We have a payment plan option as well, if you are interested.

Non-USC students are also welcome to join! The fee for non-students is $120 per semester. Contact us for more details about joining if you are not a USC student.
