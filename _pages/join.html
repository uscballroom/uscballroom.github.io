Join the Team

We welcome all USC students, alumni, and faculty, as well as Los Angeles community members. Our team is for all experience levels, from total beginners to dancing masters. No tryouts or partner needed.

How to join:

All you have to do is come to any one of our Monday or Wednesday Beginner classes. There is no commitment — your first week of classes is free!

View the Schedule

Sign up for our email newsletter for updates and schedule reminders.

If you have any questions, check the Frequently Asked Questions page, and don't hesitate to contact us.

Membership benefits:

  • Hour-long beginner and intermediate classes, 2 days a week, with professional coaches (read more about them on the leaders page)
  • Practice sessions where you can improve your dancing skills under the mentorship of experienced members
  • Carpools to social dancing events around the L.A. area
  • Participation in many fun & safe team bonding / social events
  • Discounts on dance shoes through our favorite shoe vendor, Crown Dance Shoes

Membership cost

Try out ballroom dancing for first week for free! To join the team, there is a fee of $90 per semester for USC students. This fee allows us to have our incredible professional coaches teach us every week. We have a payment plan option as well, if you are interested.

Non-USC students are also welcome to join! The fee for non-students is $130 per semester. Contact us for more details about joining if you are not a USC student.
